## Overview

[String][api.type.String] describing what happened. Can be one of the following:

* `"connected to room"`
* `"disconnected from room"`
* `"P2P connected"`
* `"P2P disconnected"`
* `"peer declined"`
* `"peer invited to room"`
* `"peer joined"`
* `"peer left"`
* `"peers connected"`
* `"peers disconnected"`
* `"room automatching"`
* `"room connecting"`
